&textHeading=Project: Chimney Road Addition
Location: Woodstock, NY
Size: Addition 1170 sf
Completed: July '09

&text=This addition provides a tower element around which all of the various building elements on the site are organized.  The tower represents the ‘core’ of the dwelling, both visually and functionally.  As a high performance structure, the addition incorporates all of the basic programmatic components for the house, allowing for expansion into seasonal spaces during the warmer months.
